Growth Lever Challenges and Advantage
Growth Dimension Description
Enter export markets Becoming an exporter can put you on a fast-track to growth, but it can be a big challenge to get started and achieve success. You must think about a host of complicated questions—everything from picking the right target market to logistics, product adaptation, pricing, customs, marketing and your company’s export-readiness.
Enter new markets with existing products Geographical expansion is an opportunity to create superior value for your business in new geography. This requires you to build an optimal go to market strategy basis the market attractiveness and operational synergies. Make sure you are not present in too many geographies and lack necessary focus before you achieve sustainable scale.
New products to existing customers Product development is an element of growth strategy where a business aims to introduce new products into existing markets. This strategy may require the development of new competencies and requires the business to develop modified products which can appeal to existing markets.
Penetrate existing markets Market Penetration Strategy, the firms focus is on selling their current products in an existing market. The firms try to focus on testing with different growth strategies to increase their market share.
